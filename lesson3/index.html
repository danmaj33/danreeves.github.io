Site Name

The name I have Chosen for my site is "Fo'Drizzle". A play on hip-hop language coined by rapper Snoop Dog, and using the litteral slang for a light rain "Drizzle".


Site Purpose

This site is intended to inform readers of the weather at various locations with a hip-hop theme. This would appeal to those who find a normal weather site boring.
