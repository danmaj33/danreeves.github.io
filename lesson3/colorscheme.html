Color Application

The colors that we are using in this site are a bright warm color scheme accented with blue (specific color codes above). We will use our blue for our headers, footers, and H3 headings. Navigation will be orange. H1, H2 Will be orange-red. Yellow and Yellow Orange will be used as background against black type.
