Target Audience

The target audience for this site are individuals 18-45 years of age who have been exposed to the culture of hip-hop and rap music and enjoy that cultural reflection. The audience will include those who live, or plan on travelling to Preston, Soda Springs , Fish Haven, Storm Center, and Gallery, Idaho.

Personas

Nick Crenshaw

Currently living in Fish Haven, Idaho. He is working there over the summer to earn money for a car. He lives in Brooklyn, New York with his mother, father and sister. He loves the music of Jay-Z, Dr. Dre and Snoop Dog. He is not familiar with Idaho's weather and wants to be up to date while living in Fish Haven.

Douglas Randle

Having just returned home from a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints in San Diego, California. Douglas has had a hard time adjusting to the differing climate of his hometown Preston, Idaho. During his mission he grew to love the hip-hop music people would listen to in his areas that he served. He desires to find a source of weather information, and in a flavor that reminds him of the people he served for two years.
