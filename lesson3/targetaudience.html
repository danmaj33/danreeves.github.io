Target Audience

The target audience for this site are individuals 18-45 years of age who have been exposed to the culture of hip-hop and rap music and enjoy that cultural reflection. The audience will include those who live, or plan on travelling to Preston, Soda Springs , Fish Haven, Storm Center, and Gallery, Idaho.

Personas


Nick Crenshaw

Currently living in Fish Haven, Idaho. He is working there over the summer to earn money for a car. He lives in Brooklyn, New York with his mother, father and sister. He loves the music of Jay-Z, Dr. Dre and Snoop Dog. He is not familiar with Idaho's weather and wants to be up to date while living in Fish Haven.



Douglas Randle

Having just returned home from a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints in San Diego, California. Douglas has had a hard time adjusting to the differing climate of his hometown Preston, Idaho. During his mission he grew to love the hip-hop music people would listen to in his areas that he served. He desires to find a source of weather information, and in a flavor that reminds him of the people he served for two years.

Scenarios

  • Douglas is interested in seeing the weather in Preston is any different in Fish Haven, will he be able to? Yes, Douglas will be able to see the weather of both places and compare.
  • Nick would like to view the website from his phone and his desktop computer at work. Will he be able to view the site easily on both? Yes, Nick will be able to see the site easily on any device Tablet, Phone, and Desktop
  • Nick's family is coming to see him from back east. They will be driving can they see multiple towns in Idaho. Yes, The website will have multiple towns to view for those to compare weather when crossing the state.
  • Douglas prefers sites that are less mainstream in their visual appeal will this be original?
    Yes, According to a major search engine finding another Idaho focused Hip-Hop weather website would be difficult.
  • Nick is having a hard time knowing what to wear each day and needs to be able to plan will information for multiple days be available? Yes, Nick will be able to see each weeks weather for Fish Haven.
