What have we done

By Daniel Reeves


The drive has last forever. Leaving Jackson ages ago, hours ago. We have been driving this road forever. I look out the window, I see the same hill, I have seen it before, I will see it again. The hill is all always there, and there and there. It sits hot high in the sun, lazy and baked. In winter it sits topped in alabaster dust a barrier no longer lazy.
